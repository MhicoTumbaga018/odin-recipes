Filipino Lumpia

My Filipino stepmother made these delicious lumpia on special occasions. You can find frozen lumpia wrappers at Asian grocery stores or use egg roll wrappers as a substitute. Serve with banana ketchup if you can find it.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package lumpia wrappers
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped onion
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped green bell pepper
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped carrot
  • 1 quart oil for frying
